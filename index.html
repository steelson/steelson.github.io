About the Game

Place cards onto a 5×5 grid and score poker hands across every row and column. Each deal raises the stakes — survive as long as you can before the board fills.

🃏

Strategic Placement

Every card you place affects up to two hands simultaneously — rows and columns. Think ahead.

♾️

Endless Survival

No rounds, no levels — just one board getting harder to manage with every card dealt.

📈

Score Multipliers

Chain strong hands to build combos. A Royal Flush at the right moment can turn the game around.

🎯

Pure Puzzle Logic

No luck in where you place — only the card dealt is random. Your strategy decides everything.
